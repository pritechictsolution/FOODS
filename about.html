Quality Food Producer in Malawi

Trusted indigenous brand providing authentic "kumudzi" taste

MAME Foods is a producer and supplier of food stuffs in Malawi. We produce and pack one of the finest and most aromatic long grained rice in Malawi as well as produce flour and different kinds of agricultural produce.

  • Our rice is pre-certified by the Malawi Bureau of Standards
  • Substantial progress towards full certification
  • All products in different phases of certification process

Our drive is to give you quality organic food stuffs that maintain their original "kumudzi" taste at a good price.
